case class HydroPowerPlant(sup: PowerSystemResource, dischargeTravelDelay: Double, genRatedP: Double, hydroPlantStorageType: String, penstockType: String, plantDischargeCapacity: Double, plantRatedHead: Double, pumpRatedP: Double, surgeTankCode: String, surgeTankCrestLevel: Double, GenSourcePumpDischargeReservoir: String, HydroGeneratingUnits: List[String], HydroPumps: List[String], Reservoir: String) extends Element with Product with Serializable

A hydro power station which can generate or pump.

When generating, the generator turbines receive water from an upper reservoir. When pumping, the pumps receive their water from a lower reservoir.
